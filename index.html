MEET OUR TEAM

John Mercado

John is a sculptor specializing in metalwork and installations. His pieces often explore themes of identity, memory, and societal norms. With a background in engineering, John brings a technical precision and innovative approach to the team, creating captivating three-dimensional artworks.

Nancy Reyes

Nancy is a mixed-media artist known for her vibrant and abstract paintings. She often incorporates recycled materials into her work, promoting sustainability and environmental awareness. Nancy's artistic vision brings a unique blend of colors and textures to the company's portfolio.

Steve Smith

Steve is a digital artist and illustrator with a passion for storytelling. His illustrations often feature whimsical characters and intricate details, drawing viewers into imaginative worlds. Steve's expertise in digital artistry adds a contemporary and versatile dimension to the company's offerings.
